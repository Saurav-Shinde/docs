## 1️⃣ Wallet Management

Inside the drawer, the admin can see the client’s wallet details:

- Current Balance
- Transaction activity (deductions)
- Due Amount Alert
- Services Provided

---

### Deduct Amount (Mandatory Reason)

Admin can deduct money from wallet when charges are applied.

Steps:

1. Click **Deduct Amount**
2. Enter amount
3. Enter reason (mandatory)
4. Confirm

Admin may deduct platform/service fees manually whenever required.

Every deduction must contain a reason.

Examples:

- Monthly subscription
- Special charges
- Adjustment entries

The system records:

- Amount deducted
- Date & time
- Reason

This ensures accountability and audit tracking.

---

### Due Alert (When Balance is Zero)

If the wallet balance becomes insufficient:

The admin can mark the amount as **Due** instead of blocking operations.

This is used when:

- Service already provided
- Order allowed on credit
- Monthly billing client

After marking due:

- Client will see pending payment
- Next recharge automatically adjusts due first

---

## 2️⃣ Service Management

Below wallet section, the admin sees **Services Panel** for that brand.

Services represent non-food operational activities provided to the client.

Examples:

- Menu engineering
- SOP creation
- Food tasting and trials
- Recipe development

---

### Add New Service

Admin can create a new service entry for the brand.

Steps:

1. Click **Add Service**
2. Enter service name
3. Save

---

### Delete Service

Admin can remove a service if no longer offered.

---

### Mark Service as Completed

When a service is provided to the client:

Admin ticks (✔) the service.

This action:

- Updates service status
- Saves record in system
- Immediately visible to client
